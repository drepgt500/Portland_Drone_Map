Drone Site Locator Map - Portland, Oregon

This map was created to assist drone pilots find ideal location for recreational drone flights and to raise awareness of sensative areas. Please use this map as a starting point for site search and follow up with local authorities.

Layer Control

Schools (Do NOT fly over public events or groups of people).
Police Stations (Do NOT fly over or near emergency response efforts).
Hospitals (Do NOT fly over or near emergency response efforts).
Obstruction Points (Stay clear of obstructions). AGL stands for Above Ground Level (reported in feet on the map).
Arenas (Do NOT fly over public events, groups of people, or stadiums full of people).
Airport Buffers - 5 miles (Must have permission to fly near airport).
